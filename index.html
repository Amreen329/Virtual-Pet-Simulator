Tips

  • Keep hunger low by feeding your pet regularly.
  • Play to increase happiness, but it will use some energy.
  • Give your pet a bath to boost its happiness and keep it fresh.
  • Use medicine when your pet looks unwell to help it recover.
  • Let your pet sleep to recover energy.
  • If hunger gets too high or happiness too low, your pet will be sad.
